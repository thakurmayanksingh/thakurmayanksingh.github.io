Get to know me better

Hello! I'm a 21-year-old with a curious, slightly sardonic heart who has a strong passion for music, animals, and the outdoors.

Perhaps it's my serene demeanor or the fact that I truly care, but I've always been the type of person who others open up to. I think it's important to support people in difficult times as well as in pleasant ones. My friends refer to me as the mature person who always puts the needs of others before their own. They're also not incorrect.

I have a special place in my heart for dogs, and if I'm by myself, you can probably find me strolling around a green space or losing myself in music. Nature is my reset button, and singing is my therapy.

Sarcasm, which keeps things light while maintaining a strong sense of groundedness, is my secret sauce since life is too short to be taken too seriously. I cherish sincere discussions, enduring friendships, and the small pleasures in life.

I, the dreamer, the listener, the artist, am reflected in this area. I appreciate you coming by. Let's collaborate, communicate, and perhaps even sing a few songs together.
